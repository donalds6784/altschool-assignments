Spicy Chicken Pilau

A fragrant East African rice dish infused with spices, chicken, and rich flavor.

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty Level: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon pilau masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cups chicken broth or water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly and soak for 15 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic, cumin seeds, cinnamon stick, and pilau masala. Stir for 1 minute.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  5. Pour in broth, add salt, and bring to a boil.
  6. Drain rice and add to the pot. Stir gently.
  7. Cover and simmer on low heat until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  8. Fluff rice with a fork and serve hot.

Tips

For extra flavor: Toast the spices before adding the chicken. You can also garnish with fresh coriander or fried onions.
